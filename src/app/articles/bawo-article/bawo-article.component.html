Background World: A Simulated Society

In one sentence, what is BaWo?

A software engine for simulating societies in games.

For gamers, BaWo is an opportunity to enjoy more content from their gamedevs, with more variety and deeper mechanics.

For gamedevs, it's a series of codefiles or a .DLL file that they can attach to their own projects and use with only a few lines of their own code, coupled with a superfast content creation tool!

Superfast Content Creation

Besides offering the functionality of simulating societies, BaWo's true strength is how it can enable people without technical expertise to mass-produce content for their games on a truly massive scale.

So how is this accomplished? It can all be summarized in three simple facts:

  • BaWo content is written in human-readable JSON files that you can literally just drop into a project folder of your choosing and voila! The game now has more content.
  • A webapp called "BaWo Content Creator" will allow you to create BaWo content files in a simple, linear and error-free process. Get more time to design!
  • BaWo comes with a completely automated AI. You tell it what your characters care about, and they will behave accordingly!
