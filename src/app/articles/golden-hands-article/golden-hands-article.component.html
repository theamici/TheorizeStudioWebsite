Strategy as never seen before

This game comes without an established genre. Surely, it's a strategy game, but of what kind? Well, it's not like your typical 4X strategy game, not like Crusader Kings, and although it makes you think of the Sims it's not really like that either. Yet, somehow it's a bit like all of them. It borrows from their spirits, and adds its own flavour of the game about industrializing human happiness.

A person and an empire

In Golden Hands you semi-randomly generate a world with 5 nations and up to thousands of characters, with each one playable by you! Here you don't make characters, you possess them, and then attempt to turn their lives around, sometimes completely. As your character you start by experiencing a great tragedy, leading to an existential awakening where you decide to dedicate your life to one goal: reshaping the world into a happy place.

Now the challenge has been set, so how will you do it? Golden Hands will offer many challenges and many paths to try, so how far you can get? Let's see!

A BaWo game

Golden Hands is based on the BaWo technology, meaning that we will be able to give you a huge, varied and deeply interconnected world, with comparatively large amounts of content that we'll also be able to add continuously into the game's lifetime.
